Welcome to Biyani Bot – Empowering Your College Journey

At Biyani Bot – a project by MASSS, we believe that every student deserves a seamless and enjoyable college experience. That's why we’ve created a state-of-the-art chatbot to assist you every step of the way. From academic guidance to campus life tips and career advice, Biyani Bot is your reliable companion available 24/7.

Why Choose Biyani Bot?

  • Instant Academic Assistance: Whether you're struggling with your coursework or need help choosing the right subjects, Biyani Bot is here to guide you.
  • Campus Life Tips: Get the best advice on how to navigate and enjoy campus life to the fullest.
  • Career Guidance: Receive tailored career advice and stay updated on the latest job opportunities and internships.
  • Personalized Support: Our chatbot is more than just a tool; it’s your personal assistant committed to your success and well-being.
