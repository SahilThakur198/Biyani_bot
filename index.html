About Us

Welcome to Biyani Bot – a project by MASSS, your one-stop destination for all things related to college life. We understand that navigating the complexities of college can be overwhelming, which is why we've developed a cutting-edge chatbot designed specifically to assist students with their college-related queries. Our chatbot is more than just an automated response system; it’s your reliable companion for academic guidance, campus life tips, career advice, and much more. Whether you need help choosing the right courses, figuring out your schedule, finding resources on campus, or simply need someone to talk to about your college experience, our chatbot is here to help 24/7. At Biyani Bot – a project by MASSS, we’re committed to using technology to enhance your educational journey. Our mission is to support students in making informed decisions, achieving academic success, and enjoying every moment of their college experience. Join our community and empower your college journey with us!
